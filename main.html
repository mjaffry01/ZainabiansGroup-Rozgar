About Us

Zainabians Group aims to empower Muslim women to lead a satisfied and religious life. Our goal is to create a platform that understands their challenges, empowers them, builds self-confidence, and provides essential life skills. Our mission is to guide the community on the path of righteousness (Sirat al-Mustaqeem), to create a positive spiritual impact, and to promote a happy and fulfilling life while standing against social injustices and preparing ourselves to contribute to the mission of our awaited savior.

How to Use the Tool

To use the Zainabians Group Business Professionals Search tool, follow these steps:

  1. Search for Professionals: Use the search bar at the top to enter a profession (e.g., Teacher, Dentist, etc.). The tool will filter through the list of professionals and show you relevant matches.
  2. View Business Owners: You will see a list of business owners highlighted separately. Each business owner has a description of the services they provide.
  3. Show All Professionals: Click on the "Show All Professionals" button to see the complete list of available professionals, regardless of their category.
  4. Contact a Professional: Once you find a professional, you can use the following options to contact them:
    • WhatsApp: Click the WhatsApp button to send a personalized message to the professional.
    • Call: Click the Call button to directly initiate a phone call.
    • Message: Click the Message button to send an SMS. You will be prompted to enter your name and phone number, which will be included in the message.
